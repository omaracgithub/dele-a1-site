Best Resources to Prepare for DELE A1 in 2026

Preparing for DELE A1 requires about 80-100 hours of study from scratch. With the right resources, you can do it in 2-3 months by dedicating one hour a day. The key is to use materials designed for absolute beginners, not for intermediate students who are reviewing.

Here is a selection of resources organized by type, all suitable for level A1.

Specific Preparation Books for DELE A1

For the exam

  • Preparación al DELE A1 (Edelsa) — The classic. Includes complete exam models with audio and solutions. Identical format to the real exam.
  • El Cronómetro A1 (Edinumen) — Focused on time management. Each task includes a suggested timer. Good strategies for each test.
  • Las claves del DELE A1 (Difusión) — Practical tips, common mistakes, and 5 complete exam models.

To learn Spanish from scratch

  • Aula Internacional 1 (Difusión) — Class manual covering all grammatical and lexical content of A1. Well-structured, with communicative activities.
  • Nuevo Prisma A1 (Edinumen) — Another complete manual with a communicative approach. Includes downloadable audio.
  • Gramática de uso del español A1-A2 (SM) — Only grammar, with clear explanations and many exercises. Perfect as a complement.

Apps for Practice

  • DELE A1 Practice — Exam-type exercises for all skills. Real format from Instituto Cervantes with automatic correction.
  • Duolingo — Useful for basic vocabulary and daily practice of 5-10 minutes. Does not directly prepare for the exam but builds lexical base.
  • Anki — Flashcards with spaced repetition. Search for "DELE A1 vocabulary" decks already created by other users. Ideal for memorizing articles with nouns.
  • SpanishDict — Dictionary with verb conjugator, pronunciation, and examples. Essential as a quick reference.

YouTube Channels for Absolute Beginners

  • SpanishPod101 (Absolute Beginner level) — Short videos with very basic dialogues, subtitles, and explanations in English. Good for the first weeks.
  • Butterfly Spanish — Explanations of A1 grammar in English with clear examples. Pronunciation, numbers, basic verbs, articles.
  • Spanish with Vicente — Short videos on specific A1 topics: introducing oneself, numbers, family, home. Speaks slowly and with subtitles.
  • Hola Spanish — Structured course from scratch with numbered lessons. You can follow it like a textbook in video format.

Podcasts for Level A1

At level A1, you need podcasts designed for absolute beginners. Podcasts "for Spanish students" are usually level B1+. Look specifically for those that say "beginner" or "total beginner."

  • News in Slow Spanish (Beginner) — Simplified news at a very slow speed. The "beginner" level is appropriate for advanced A1.
  • SpanishPod101 (Absolute Beginner) — Episodes of 5-10 minutes with very short dialogues and explanations in English.
  • Coffee Break Spanish (season 1) — Spanish course in podcast format. The first season starts from absolute zero.
  • Español con Juan (episodes for beginners) — Juan speaks very slowly and clearly. Look for his videos marked as "basic level."

Official Resources from Instituto Cervantes

  • Exam Models — Instituto Cervantes publishes free models on its website. They are the most reliable resource because they have exactly the format of the real exam.
  • AVE Global — Online platform from Cervantes with Spanish courses by levels. Level A1 includes interactive exercises and tests.
  • Guía del candidato DELE A1 — Free PDF document explaining the exam structure, evaluation criteria, and general tips.

Tips for Choosing Resources

  • Do not use more than 2-3 resources at a time. It’s better to finish one book than to start five.
  • Make sure the resource says "A1" or "absolute beginner." A book on "Spanish for foreigners" without a level may be too advanced.
  • Always combine an input resource (reading/listening) with an output resource (writing/speaking).
  • Official exam models are mandatory. Do at least 3 complete exams before the actual day.
  • Consistency beats intensity. 30 minutes every day is better than 4 hours on Saturday.

Practical Exam Information

  • Price: approximately €112 in Spain (varies by country).
  • Sessions: several per year (check dates at Instituto Cervantes).
  • Result: pass or fail. You need at least 30/50 in each group of tests.
  • DELE A1 is not valid for applying for Spanish nationality (A2 is required at minimum).
